Black Jaguar

Jaguars are competent climbers, and will scale trees. They often use their
vantage point among the branches to pounce on unsuspecting prey below.

  • Scientific Name: Black Jaguar
  • Average Weight: 56 to 96 kg (adult)
  • Average Length:63 to 76 cm (adult)
  • Average Lifespan: 12 to 15 years in the wild
  • Habitat: Americas

A black panther is the melanistic color variant of any Panthera species.
Black panthers in Asia and Africa are leopards (Panthera pardus)
and black panthers in the Americas are black jaguars (Panthera onca).
